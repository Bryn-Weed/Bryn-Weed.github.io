Interests

After working with a couple talented designers, I've recently developed an interest in design beyond the typical web development scope. I'm currently investigating the world of SVGs, while also looking into the techniques used to create graphical artwork that I may, some day, be able to implement in my own work.

I believe that there can be beauty in simplicity; you don't need to have a lot going on in a site for it to look good, and sometimes less is better.
